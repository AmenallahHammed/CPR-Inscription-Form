CPR Saves Lives

Cardiac arrest can happen anywhere, anytime. Performing CPR within the first few minutes can double or triple survival rates. Learn how to act fast and make a difference!

Hands-Only CPR

No mouth-to-mouth? No problem! Hands-Only CPR is just as effective. Push hard and fast in the center of the chest to keep the heart beating until help arrives.

CPR & AED

An AED (Automated External Defibrillator) can restore a normal heartbeat. If available, use it alongside CPR to maximize survival chances.
