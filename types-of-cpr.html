Hands-Only CPR

Hands-Only CPR is simple and effective for sudden cardiac arrest. It involves pushing hard and fast in the center of the chest at a rate of 100-120 compressions per minute.

Traditional CPR (With Rescue Breaths)

Traditional CPR combines chest compressions and rescue breaths to provide oxygen to the brain and keep the heart pumping.

CPR with an AED (Defibrillator Use)

An AED is a life-saving device that delivers an electric shock to restore a normal heartbeat.
