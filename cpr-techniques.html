CPR Techniques

Adult CPR Technique

1. Check if the person is unresponsive.
2. Call emergency services (911).
3. Start 30 chest compressions (press hard & fast, 2-inch depth).
4. Give 2 rescue breaths (tilt head, pinch nose, breathe in).
5. Repeat until help arrives.

Child & Infant CPR

- **Children (1-12 years)** → Use one hand for compressions (2-inch depth).
- **Infants (under 1 year)** → Use two fingers for compressions (1.5-inch depth).
- Both need 30 compressions + 2 rescue breaths.

CPR Timing & Rhythm

Performing CPR at the correct pace is critical. The recommended rate is **100-120 compressions per minute**—the same rhythm as songs like *Stayin’ Alive* by the Bee Gees.
